# Routine Vehicle Maintenance Checklist

A simple guide to keeping your automobile running smoothly for years.

## Weekly Checks
- Check the pressure on all four tires and ensure they meet the manufacturer's recommended PSI.
- Inspect the tread depth.
- Clean the windshield wipers and top up the washer fluid.

## Every 5,000 Miles
- Change the engine oil and replace the oil filter. Dirty oil is the fastest way to ruin a motor.
- Rotate the tires to ensure even wear across the treads.
- Inspect the brake pads for wear and tear.

## Every 30,000 Miles
- Flush the transmission fluid.
- Replace the engine air filter and the cabin air filter.
- Check the battery terminals for corrosion.

Taking care of your vehicle ensures you don't break down on the motorway when you least expect it. Preventative care is much cheaper than replacing an entire engine block!
