# Review: L'Oven on Main Street

Saved from Yelp.

Honestly, one of the best weekend spots in the city. The aroma hits you before you even open the door.

We started with the pain au chocolat, which was perfectly flaky and had the perfect ratio of dark chocolate. The sourdough boules coming out of the kitchen looked incredible, with a beautiful dark crust and an airy, open crumb. We also picked up a few traditional baguettes for a dinner party later. 

If you go, make sure you arrive before 9 AM on a Saturday, because the queue wraps around the block and they often sell out of the almond croissants.
