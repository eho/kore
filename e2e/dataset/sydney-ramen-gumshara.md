# Gumshara Ramen — Sydney

Saved from broadsheet.com.au.

Located right in the heart of Haymarket, Sydney, inside the Eating World food court. 

Gumshara is famous for having what might be the thickest, richest tonkotsu broth outside of Japan. Chef Mori produces the soup by boiling over 100kg of pork bones daily. The collagen-heavy broth is so dense it almost acts like a gravy rather than a soup.

**Details:**
- Very casual food court setting.
- Usually a massive line during lunch and dinner.
- Try the Garlic Tonkotsu or the spare rib ramen.
- Not for the faint-hearted—it is extremely heavy and filling.

An absolute institution for Sydney foodies.
