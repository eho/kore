# Classic Apple Pie Recipe

From Grandma's recipe book.

A perfect autumn dessert. The secret is getting the right mix of sweet and tart fruit.

## Ingredients

**For the filling:**
- 6 cups Granny Smith and Honeycrisp apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

**For the crust:**
- 2 store-bought pie crusts (or homemade if you have time)
- 1 egg (for egg wash)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
2. In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples with sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice.
3. Unroll one pie crust and press it into a 9-inch pie dish.
4. Pour the apple filling into the crust.
5. Cover with the second crust. Crimp the edges to seal.
6. Brush the top with beaten egg and cut a few slits to allow steam to escape.
7. Bake for 45-50 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream.
