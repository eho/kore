Chocolate Cake

[**Chocolate Cake**](https://cafedelites.com/chocolate-cake/)

## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 cup strong black coffee
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2 tsp vanilla extract

![](../attachments/cake-photo.jpg)

## Method

Preheat oven to 175°C. Mix dry ingredients. Whisk wet ingredients separately then combine.
